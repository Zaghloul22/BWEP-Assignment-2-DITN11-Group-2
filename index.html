How to make an E-Card.


1. Start with presets

We hook you up with a couple of designed template

so you’re never starting from a blank canvas.

Once you find a graphic to start from,

just tap or click to edit the template.



2. Remix it to make it your own

You can customize the texts in the given template.

For example, the event name , address and the date when the event will occur.



3. Enjoy your newly made E-Card!

Now that you have made your own E-Card,

You are now able to send to your friends and families



The customization doesn’t stop there.

You can customize your own E-Card,

By using the drag-and-drop feature, you can

customize what wallpaper and type of E-Card you

want to make
